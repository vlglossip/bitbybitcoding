Become an Ambassador

Lead with Purpose. Build with Vision.

As a Bit by Bit Ambassador, you’ll launch and lead a Bit by Bit chapter at your school or in your community, guiding peers through our yearlong coding curriculum.

We’ll equip you with the curriculum, tools, and support you need. Your chapter will have the opportunity to compete as a team in our global hackathons, turning ideas into real-world impact. Every few months, you’ll submit a brief check-in.

Our curriculum is designed to introduce coding to learners of all ages: from curious upper elementary school students to motivated teens and adults looking to dive into tech.

You’re not just joining a program, you’re starting a movement.
