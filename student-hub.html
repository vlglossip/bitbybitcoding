Become a Bit by Bit Student

Learn with Purpose. Build with Confidence.

At Bit by Bit, you're not just learning to code: you're discovering how to think critically, solve real-world problems, and create technology that makes a difference.

Our program is open to students ages 7 and up, and offers six dynamic courses designed to grow with you. Whether you're just starting out or ready to build your first app, there's a class for you.

If you're looking to learn HTML and CSS, take one of our Web Development courses.

If you're looking to learn JavaScript, take one of our App Development courses.

If you're proficient in HTML and CSS, sign up for one of our Web Development hackathons.

If you're proficient in JavaScript, sign up for one of our App Development hackathons.
