Our Team

Founder | Chief Executive Officer

Victoria Glossip

Victoria Glossip is the founder and CEO of Bit by Bit Coding, an award-winning advocate for computer science education, and a passionate leader dedicated to making tech accessible for all. As an NCWIT Impact Award recipient and the youngest Junior Developer Intern at Farragut Systems, she has worked on cutting-edge projects in web development, data analytics, and automation. Beyond her technical expertise, Victoria has expanded coding education access globally, reaching thousands of students through free courses, workshops, and mentorship. She is a global WiSTEM ambassador, a published author of A Girl’s Blueprint to Building Websites, and the creator of "Bit by Bit in a Kit," a resource empowering students to start coding clubs worldwide. Victoria is pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Statistics, with the goal of shaping the future of technology through innovation, education, and advocacy. Through Bit by Bit Coding, she continues to inspire and equip the next generation of programmers—bit by bit.

Our Mission

At Bit by Bit Coding, we believe that coding is more than just a technical skill—it’s a tool for empowerment. Our mission is to provide free, high-quality programming education to students worldwide, breaking down barriers of geography, gender, and socioeconomic status. We are committed to: Expanding access to coding education through free courses, workshops, and mentorship. Creating inclusive learning opportunities, particularly for young girls in STEM. Offering flexible learning models, including recorded sessions and self-paced programs, to ensure that every student has the opportunity to succeed. Supporting the next generation of leaders by providing guidance in both technical and leadership skills. Technology has the power to change lives, and at Bit by Bit Coding, we are dedicated to making that change a reality—one lesson, one student, and one breakthrough at a time.

Our Story

Bit by Bit Coding was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to make programming education accessible to all. Our journey began when our founder, an NCWIT Impact Award-winning advocate for computer science, saw firsthand the barriers that prevent students—especially young girls and international learners—from accessing quality coding education. Starting as a personal mission to bridge this gap, Bit by Bit Coding has grown into a global initiative. From authoring A Girl’s Blueprint to Building Websites to launching a YouTube channel and middle school clubs, our founder has worked tirelessly to empower the next generation of programmers. Through our efforts, we’ve reached over 4,500 individuals, offering free coding lessons and hands-on expereinces.. Bit by Bit Coding continues to grow, expanding into new initiatives like "Bit by Bit in a Kit," which equips students with the resources to start their own coding clubs. With every lesson taught and every student empowered, we are proving that anyone, anywhere, can learn to code—bit by bit.
