Our Mission

Bit by Bit Coding was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to make programming education accessible to all. Our journey began when our founder, an NCWIT National Impact Award-winning advocate for computer science, experienced firsthand the barriers that prevent students from accessing quality coding education. Our vision is that our students will use this newfound knowledge to solve problems in their own communities, spreading Bit by Bit Coding's mission beyond the classroom.
