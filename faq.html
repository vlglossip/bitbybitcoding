Frequently Asked Questions

Are Bit by Bit classes free?

Yes! Our classes are completely free! We are committed to making coding education accessible to everyone.

What do I do if we can't attend a Welcome Session?

No worries, we'll send out a recap of all the information covered at our welcome session.

Can I join without having previous experience and/or knowledge?

Absolutely! Our coding organization welcomes beginners and provides a supportive learning environment. We offer classes designed to teach foundational coding skills, allowing you to learn at your own pace and build your knowledge from the ground up.

Where is my meeting link for an online class?

We connect over zoom. The link will be emailed 10 minutes before class begins.

Who will be my coaches?

Your coaches will be experienced mentors who are passionate about coding and education. They come from diverse backgrounds, including professional developers, educators, and skilled volunteers, all dedicated to helping you succeed. Our coaches provide guidance, support, and real-world insights to make your learning experience engaging and effective.

If I already have basic knowledge, is it worth studying at Bit by Bit Coding?

Yes, studying at Bit by Bit Coding is definitely worth it, even if you already have basic knowledge. Our programs are designed to deepen your understanding of coding concepts and introduce you to more advanced topics. You'll have the opportunity to work on real projects, collaborate with peers, and receive guidance from experienced coaches. This can enhance your skills and confidence, helping you to become a more proficient coder and prepare you for future challenges in the tech world!

What programming languages do you teach?

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Do I have to do any homework in between sessions?

While homework is not mandatory, we do encourage practice between sessions to reinforce what you've learned. Some courses may include optional exercises or projects to help you apply concepts and build your skills. Completing these can enhance your learning experience and better prepare you for future lessons.

Do you offer a hackathon?

Yes! Our hackathons provide a fun and collaborative environment for participants to work on coding projects, solve challenges, and showcase their skills. It’s a great opportunity to network with fellow coders, learn new techniques, and apply what you've learned in a hands-on setting. Keep an eye out for announcements regarding upcoming hackathons, and we hope you’ll join us for an exciting experience!

Will I earn a certificate?

Yes! You will receive a certificate upon successfully completing each Hackathon Prep Course as a recognition of your achievement.

Additionally, after participating in a Hackathon, you will earn a certificate and have the opportunity to win an award!

Can I attend a hackathon without attending a hackathon prep course?

No, you must attend 1 hackathon prep course at the level you wish to compete at.
